About

Digital Product Designer • UI/UX Designer • Mobile App Designer • Web Designer

Hi, I'm Max Batista (MrBanika), a Digital Product Designer and UI/UX Designer specializing in creating user-centered digital experiences that combine functionality with emotional impact.

My approach blends product thinking with visual storytelling—transforming complex user needs into intuitive, visually compelling interfaces. With expertise in UI/UX, web development, and a background in IT support, I bridge the gap between technical feasibility and human-centered design.

My work as a visual artist fuels my design process, bringing a unique sensitivity to composition, color, and narrative that makes digital products not just usable—but memorable.

When I'm not designing, I'm creating digital art, exploring music, and experimenting with visual concepts that keep my creative practice evolving.
